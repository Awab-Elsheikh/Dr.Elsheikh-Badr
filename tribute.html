Dr. Elsheikh Badr

A successful man and a great father

Here's a time line of Dr. Elsheikh academic journey:

  • 1996 - Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery - University of Gezira 1996.
  • 1998 - Higher Diploma in Public Health - Faculty of Medicine, University of Gezira.
  • 2000 - Fellowship of the Sudanese National Council for Medical Specialties - Community Medicine.
  • 2005 - Master of Health Policy, Planning and Management - University of Leeds - United Kingdom.
  • 2010 - Fellowship of the Royal College of Physicians - Public Health - London - United Kingdom.

Achievements

  • Federal Ministry of Health 2001 - 2007: Prepared national health policies and plans, built the capacities of the states and the staff, established the National Observatory, and prepared a policy for doctor migration.
  • Academy of Health Sciences 2008 - 2013: Established the academy and its branches, transformed it into a university, and graduated many health professionals.
  • Sudan Doctors Union 2009 - 2014: Built the professional complex, contributed to the Central Doctor Hospital, and established a branch of the Arab Institute.
  • World Health Organization 2013 - 2014: Developed a strategy and provided assistance for human health resources in the region.
  • Medical Specialties Council 2014 - 2018: Improved the council's infrastructure, resources, specialties, recognition, and scope.

"Dr. Elsheikh (my father) is a great achieving man and a man with high morals and honour, and of course a man whom I look up to."

-- Awab Elsheikh.
